## What is VOLA?

VOLA is a suite of innovative financial products running on HyperEVM.

Our goal is to empower users to precisely control their risk exposure and leverage, all while maintaining exceptional capital efficiency and eliminating liquidation risks.

## Popular Use-Cases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Risk-Free Leverage">
    Use HYPE.v for leveraged exposure to HYPE without liquidation risk or ongoing funding fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stable Asset Creation">
    Mint USD.hype to transform volatile assets into stable USD exposure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customized Risk Profile">
    Mint any ratio of USD.hype/HYPE.v to precisely control your market exposure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stable Yield Farming">
    Use USD.hype in DeFi protocols to earn yield without exposure to underlying asset volatility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dual Asset Strategy">
    Hold both USD.hype and HYPE.v to maintain original asset exposure while unlocking liquidity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Liquid Staking">

  </Step>
