## Base Staking Yield

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Reward Mechanism">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Network Rewards">
        The HYPE network generates staking rewards at approximately 2.9% APY based on the current distribution parameters.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Validator Collection">
        VOLA Protocol's validators collect these rewards on behalf of all hbHYPE holders.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Value Accrual">
        Instead of distributing tokens directly, rewards accrue into the value of hbHYPE against HYPE.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Exchange Rate Growth">
        The hbHYPE/HYPE exchange rate increases over time, reflecting accumulated rewards.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Note>
      This accumulation approach eliminates the need for manual claims and automatically compounds returns.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Yield Calculation">
    <div className="mb-4">
      The base staking yield accumulates through the appreciation of the hbHYPE/HYPE exchange rate:
    </div>
    
    <Card>
      **Exchange Rate Appreciation Formula:**
      
      If starting exchange rate is 1 hbHYPE = 1 HYPE:
      
      ```
      Exchange Rate After 1 Year = 1 × (1 + Annual APY)
      ```
      
      With 2.9% APY, after 1 year: 1 hbHYPE ≈ 1.029 HYPE
    </Card>
    
    <div className="mt-4">
      This means that if you initially convert 1,000 HYPE to 1,000 hbHYPE, after one year of holding:
    </div>
    
    - Your balance: Still 1,000 hbHYPE
    - Value when redeemed: ~1,029 HYPE
    - Net yield: ~2.9% in HYPE terms
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Ecosystem Airdrops

<Card>
  A significant potential yield source for hbHYPE holders comes from ecosystem airdrops captured through VOLA's strategic validator distribution.
</Card>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Airdrop Qualification">
    Many projects in the Hyperliquid ecosystem distribute airdrops to stakers, often filtered by validator participation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Strategic Distribution">
    VOLA Protocol distributes staked HYPE across a carefully selected set of validators to maximize airdrop capture potential.
  </Step>
  <Step title="100% Pass-Through">
    All captured airdrops are passed through to hbHYPE holders with zero management fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Distribution Mechanism">
    Airdropped tokens are distributed to hbHYPE holders proportionally to their holdings at the time of the snapshot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  While historical data from comparable ecosystems suggests airdrops can significantly enhance overall yield, future airdrop rewards are inherently unpredictable and should not be considered guaranteed.
</Warning>

## Yield Accrual Mechanism

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Exchange Rate">
    When hbHYPE is launched, 1 hbHYPE = 1 HYPE
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reward Collection">
    As staking rewards are generated, they are collected by the protocol
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exchange Rate Adjustment">
    Instead of distributing rewards, the hbHYPE/HYPE exchange rate increases
  </Step>
  <Step title="Value Growth">
    The value of hbHYPE in HYPE terms grows over time, reflecting accumulated rewards
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card>
  **Example:**
  
  1. Day 0: You stake 1,000 HYPE and receive 1,000 hbHYPE (1:1 ratio)
  2. After 6 months: Exchange rate grows to 1 hbHYPE = 1.0145 HYPE (reflecting ~2.9% APY)
  3. Your 1,000 hbHYPE is now worth 1,014.5 HYPE
  4. After 1 year: Exchange rate reaches 1 hbHYPE = 1.029 HYPE
  5. Your 1,000 hbHYPE is now worth 1,029 HYPE
</Card>
