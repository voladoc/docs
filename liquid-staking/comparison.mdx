## Detailed Comparisons

### Liquidity and Capital Efficiency

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Direct Staking">
    <Frame>
      ![Direct Staking](/images/direct-staking.png)
    </Frame>
    
    **Liquidity Profile:**
    - HYPE tokens are fully bonded to validators
    - Assets remain locked during the entire staking period
    - Unbonding requires a ~7 day waiting period
    - No capital efficiency or DeFi integrations
    
    **Capital Utility:** Low - Your capital works in only one way (earning staking rewards)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Custodial Staking">
    <Frame>
      ![Custodial Staking](/images/custodial-staking.png)
    </Frame>
    
    **Liquidity Profile:**
    - Varies by platform
    - Some offer "instant unstaking" at a penalty
    - Often restricted by platform rules and limits
    - Limited DeFi integrations
    
    **Capital Utility:** Low to Medium - Dependent on platform features
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="hbHYPE Liquid Staking">
    <Frame>
      ![hbHYPE Liquid Staking](/images/liquid-staking.png)
    </Frame>
    
    **Liquidity Profile:**
    - Receive hbHYPE tokens representing staked position
    - Tokens are fully transferable and tradable
    - Can be deployed across DeFi for additional yield
    - Full composability with other protocols
    
    **Capital Utility:** High - Your capital works in multiple ways simultaneously
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Risk Comparison

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Direct Staking Risks">
    - **Validator Risk**: Entire stake is dependent on one or few validators
    - **Slashing Risk**: Full exposure to validator slashing events
    - **Selection Risk**: Poor validator selection can lead to suboptimal returns
    - **Monitoring Burden**: Requires ongoing vigilance to ensure validator performance
    - **Opportunity Cost**: Capital is locked and unavailable for other opportunities
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Custodial Staking Risks">
    - **Counterparty Risk**: Full exposure to platform solvency and security
    - **Custody Risk**: Platform controls your assets
    - **Transparency Risk**: Limited visibility into actual staking operations
    - **Regulatory Risk**: Platform may be subject to regulatory actions
    - **Fee Risk**: Platform may change fee structure without notice
    - **Withdrawal Risk**: Platform may impose restrictions on withdrawals
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="hbHYPE Liquid Staking Risks">
    - **Smart Contract Risk**: Exposure to potential smart contract vulnerabilities
    - **Validator Diversification**: Risk spread across multiple validators
    - **Market Risk**: hbHYPE/HYPE exchange rate may fluctuate
    - **Slashing Risk**: Reduced through diversification but not eliminated
    - **Protocol Risk**: Dependence on VOLA Protocol's operations
    
    *All risks are actively managed through security audits, diversification strategies, and continuous monitoring.*
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  All staking methods involve some degree of risk. Users should understand these risks and choose the approach that best matches their risk tolerance and goals.
</Warning>
