## Unlocked Liquidity

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Traditional Staking">
    <div className="mb-4 text-red-500 font-semibold">Limitations:</div>
    
    - HYPE tokens are locked during the staking period
    - Unstaking requires a 7-day unbonding period
    - Capital cannot be deployed elsewhere while staking
    - Emergency access to funds is restricted
    
    <Warning>
      With traditional staking, your capital remains inaccessible until the unbonding period completes, limiting your ability to respond to market opportunities or emergency needs.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Liquid Staking">
    <div className="mb-4 text-green-500 font-semibold">Advantages:</div>
    
    - Receive hbHYPE tokens representing your staked position
    - hbHYPE tokens remain freely transferable and tradable
    - Access liquidity immediately through secondary markets
    - Maintain staking exposure while utilizing capital elsewhere
    
    <Note>
      With liquid staking, you continue earning staking rewards while maintaining the flexibility to use your assets in other ways. This dual benefit significantly enhances capital efficiency.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
