## Exchange Rate Mechanism

<div className="mb-6">
  <p className="text-lg">
    The hbHYPE token uses an accumulative (non-rebasing) exchange rate model:
  </p>
</div>

<Card className="bg-blue-50/30 dark:bg-blue-900/20 shadow-md my-6">
  <div className="font-semibold text-lg mb-3">Exchange Rate Calculation:</div>
  
  <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-4 rounded-md font-mono text-center my-4">
    hbHYPE/HYPE Exchange Rate = Total HYPE Managed by Protocol / Total hbHYPE Supply
  </div>
  
  <p>This rate increases as staking rewards accrue, meaning each hbHYPE token becomes worth more HYPE over time.</p>
</Card>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Initial State">
    <div className="p-4">
      <p className="mb-3">When the protocol launches:</p>
      
      <ul className="space-y-2 list-disc pl-6">
        <li>1 hbHYPE = 1 HYPE (initial 1:1 ratio)</li>
        <li>User deposits 100 HYPE → Receives 100 hbHYPE</li>
        <li>Total HYPE managed: 100</li>
        <li>Total hbHYPE supply: 100</li>
        <li>Exchange rate: 1.0</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="After Rewards">
    <div className="p-4">
      <p className="mb-3">After staking rewards accrue:</p>
      
      <ul className="space-y-2 list-disc pl-6">
        <li>Protocol now manages 103 HYPE (original 100 + 3 in rewards)</li>
        <li>Total hbHYPE supply remains 100</li>
        <li>New exchange rate: 103/100 = 1.03</li>
        <li>1 hbHYPE is now worth 1.03 HYPE</li>
        <li>User's 100 hbHYPE is now worth 103 HYPE</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Deposit After Rewards">
    <div className="p-4">
      <p className="mb-3">New user deposits after rewards have accrued:</p>
      
      <ul className="space-y-2 list-disc pl-6">
        <li>Current exchange rate is 1.03</li>
        <li>User deposits 100 HYPE</li>
        <li>User receives 100/1.03 = 97.09 hbHYPE</li>
        <li>This 97.09 hbHYPE is worth 100 HYPE at current rate</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note className="my-6">
  <div className="font-medium">Important Note:</div>
  <p>This token model means hbHYPE balances remain constant while their value in HYPE increases over time, simplifying integration with other DeFi protocols compared to rebasing tokens.</p>
</Note>

## Deposit and Minting Process

<div className="bg-gradient-to-r from-gray-50 to-gray-100 dark:from-gray-800/50 dark:to-gray-900/50 p-6 rounded-lg my-8">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="User Initiates Deposit">
      <p>User connects wallet to the VOLA App and specifies HYPE amount to stake.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Smart Contract Interaction">
      <p>User approves the HYPE token transfer and confirms the staking transaction.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Exchange Rate Check">
      <p>Smart contract retrieves the current hbHYPE/HYPE exchange rate.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="hbHYPE Calculation">
      <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-3 rounded-md font-mono my-2">
        hbHYPE to mint = HYPE deposited / current exchange rate
      </div>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Minting">
      <p>Protocol mints the calculated hbHYPE amount and transfers it to the user.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Stake Distribution">
      <p>Protocol distributes the deposited HYPE across validators according to the allocation strategy.</p>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>

## Redemption and Unbonding

<div className="mb-6">
  <p className="text-lg">
    The redemption process handles the conversion of hbHYPE back to HYPE:
  </p>
</div>

<div className="bg-gradient-to-r from-gray-50 to-gray-100 dark:from-gray-800/50 dark:to-gray-900/50 p-6 rounded-lg my-8">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="User Initiates Redemption">
      <p>User specifies amount of hbHYPE to redeem for HYPE.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="hbHYPE Burning">
      <p>The specified hbHYPE tokens are burned from user's wallet.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="HYPE Calculation">
      <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-3 rounded-md font-mono my-2">
        HYPE to receive = hbHYPE redeemed × current exchange rate
      </div>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Unbonding Process">
      <p>Protocol initiates unbonding of equivalent HYPE from validators.</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Waiting Period">
      <p>User waits through the Hyperliquid network's mandatory unbonding period (~7 days).</p>
    </Step>
    <Step title="HYPE Distribution">
      <p>After the unbonding period, HYPE is transferred to the user's wallet.</p>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
