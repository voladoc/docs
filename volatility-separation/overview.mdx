## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit Collateral">
    <p>Users deposit sHYPE as collateral into the Wave Protocol.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mint Financial Instruments">
    <p>The protocol mints USD.hype and/or HYPE.v tokens based on the user's preference.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Volatility Separation">
    <div>
      <p>USD.hype absorbs none of the price volatility (stable at $1)</p>
      <p>HYPE.v absorbs all of the price volatility (with floating leverage)</p>
    </div>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automated Risk Management">
    <p>The protocol continuously manages the system's Collateralization Ratio and dynamically adjusts HYPE.v's leverage.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redemption">
    <p>Users can redeem their USD.hype and/or HYPE.v for the underlying sHYPE at any time.</p>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<div className="my-12">
  <h2 className="text-2xl font-bold mb-6">Core Mechanisms</h2>

  <div className="space-y-8">
    <div>
      <h3 className="text-xl font-semibold mb-4">Value Invariant Principle</h3>
      
      <p className="mb-4">
        The foundation of the Volatility Separation Engine is a mathematical invariant ensuring that the total value of all synthetic assets (USD.hype and HYPE.v) equals the total value of sHYPE collateral:
      </p>
      
      <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-4 rounded-md font-mono text-center my-4">
        sHYPE Total Value ($) = (USD.hype Total Supply × $1) + (HYPE.v Total Supply × HYPE.v Price)
      </div>
      
      <p>
        This invariant guarantees that the system remains fully collateralized at all times, while providing the mathematical basis for transferring volatility from USD.hype to HYPE.v.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div>
      <h3 className="text-xl font-semibold mb-4">Dynamic Leverage Mechanism</h3>
      
      <p className="mb-4">
        HYPE.v features a dynamic leverage multiple that adjusts automatically based on the protocol's Collateralization Ratio (CR):
      </p>
      
      <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-4 rounded-md font-mono text-center my-4">
        Leverage (L) = 1 + (1 / (CR - 100%))
      </div>
      
      <Tabs>
        <Tab title="Concept">
          <div className="p-4">
            <p className="mb-4">As the system's Collateralization Ratio changes, HYPE.v's leverage automatically adjusts:</p>
            
            <ul className="space-y-2 list-disc pl-6">
              <li><strong>Higher CR</strong>: Lower leverage, lower risk, lower potential returns</li>
              <li><strong>Lower CR</strong>: Higher leverage, higher risk, higher potential returns</li>
            </ul>
            
            <p className="mt-4">This mechanism ensures the system remains solvent while distributing risk appropriately.</p>
          </div>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Example">
          <div className="p-4">
            <p className="mb-4">If the Collateralization Ratio is 200%:</p>
            
            <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-3 rounded-md font-mono my-4">
              Leverage = 1 + (1 / (200% - 100%)) = 1 + (1 / 100%) = 1 + 1 = 2x
            </div>
            
            <p className="mb-4">At 200% CR, HYPE.v offers 2x leverage. If sHYPE price increases by 10%, HYPE.v value would increase by approximately 20%.</p>
            
            <p className="mb-4">If CR decreases to 150%:</p>
            
            <div className="bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 p-3 rounded-md font-mono my-4">
              Leverage = 1 + (1 / (150% - 100%)) = 1 + (1 / 50%) = 1 + 2 = 3x
            </div>
            
            <p>At 150% CR, HYPE.v now offers 3x leverage. The same 10% sHYPE price increase would result in approximately 30% gain for HYPE.v.</p>
          </div>
        </Tab>
      </Tabs>
    </div>
