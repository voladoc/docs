    | Reporting Frequency | Monthly | How often grantees must report progress |
    | Retroactive Funding | Allowed | Whether post-completion funding is permitted |
  </Column>
</Columns>

<Note>
  Grant program parameters ensure efficient and transparent distribution of ecosystem funding to maximize protocol growth and community engagement.
